## Maintainer Responsibilities Reference

The following summarizes the operational responsibilities of each maintainer role. The full definitions are in GOVERNANCE.md.

**Lead Maintainer:**

- Casts tiebreaking votes on evenly divided council decisions
- Serves as external contact point for institutional partners, press, and security researchers following SECURITY.md
- Signs release tags
- Maintains this document

**Maintainer:**

- Reviews and approves pull requests within their domain
- Triages new issues within 5 business days
- Participates in Maintainer Council votes on significant decisions
- Administers releases (Beta creation, quality gate confirmation)
- Enforces CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md

**Senior Technical Reviewer:**

- Provides required sign-off on domain-specific module content changes
- Available for technical arbitration of disputed content accuracy claims
- Conducts periodic technical accuracy audits of assigned modules (at major release cycles)

## Becoming a Maintainer

A contributor becomes eligible for Maintainer consideration after meeting all of the following criteria:

- Six months of active contribution to the repository
- At least ten accepted pull requests with positive review history
- Technical content contributions that have passed Senior Technical Reviewer sign-off in at least one domain
- No confirmed violations of CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md
- Demonstrated understanding of the program's ethical framework through submitted content

Eligible contributors may be nominated by any current Maintainer. Nomination is discussed in a closed Maintainer Council session and decided by majority vote. The nominee is informed of the decision within 7 days of the vote.

## Maintainer Availability and Response Standards

Maintainers are expected to:

| Activity | Response Standard |
|---------|-----------------|
| Issue triage | Within 5 business days of opening |
| Pull request initial review | Within 7 business days of submission |
| Code of Conduct report acknowledgement | Within 48 hours |
| Governance vote participation | Within the defined comment period |

Maintainers who are unavailable for more than 30 consecutive days should notify the Lead Maintainer. Maintainers who are unreachable for more than 60 consecutive days without notice may be moved to Emeritus status by Maintainer Council majority vote.
